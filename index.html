About EcoMobileWash


EcoMobileWash offers eco-friendly mobile detailing services for a wide range of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, RVs, boats, jet skis, and airplanes. As a sustainable technology company based in San Diego, our goal is to provide top-notch, customer-centric detailing services that promote environmental responsibility.

Our EPA-approved detailing solutions are designed to minimize waste and environmental impact, as they generate no wastewater runoff and leave no stains on the ground. Our innovative waterless washing technology enables us to conserve water, using only 1% of the water compared to a conventional car wash. This results in an average savings of 40 gallons of water per car wash.

Eco Mobile Wash proudly sponsors AppChurch Global Foundation for its endeavor of closing the digital divide between Christianity and our time.
