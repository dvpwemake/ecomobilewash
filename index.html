About EcoMobileWash

EcoMobileWash offers eco-friendly mobile detailing services for a wide range of vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, RVs, boats, jetskis, and airplanes. As a sustainable technology company based in San Diego, our goal is to provide top-notch, customer-centric detailing services that promote environmental responsibility.

Our EPA approved detailing solutions are designed to minimize waste and environmental impact, as they generate no waste water run-off and leave no stains on the ground. Our innovative waterless washing technology enables us to conserve water, using only 5% of the amount typically required for a traditional carwash. This results in an average savings of 40 gallons of water per carwash, further demonstrating our commitment to sustainability.
